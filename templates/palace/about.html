The Seat of Ankpa Kingdom

{% if palace_info.about_text %}
{{ palace_info.about_text|linebreaks }}
{% else %}

The Ejeh Ankpa Palace stands as the traditional seat of power and cultural heritage for the Igala people of Ankpa in Kogi State, Nigeria. For generations, this institution has preserved the customs, traditions, and identity of our people.

The Ejeh of Ankpa serves as the paramount traditional ruler, custodian of culture, and father to all citizens. The palace remains a symbol of unity, peace, and continuity of our cherished traditions.
